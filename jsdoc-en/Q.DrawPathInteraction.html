Q.DrawPathInteraction

new DrawPathInteraction(graph)

Create a path interactor, inheriting from Q.DrawableInteraction to realize the route drawing during the interactive process. Creation of the link interaction Q.CreateEdgeInteraction and the creation of the path interaction Q.CreateShapeInteraction are both inherit from this class.
